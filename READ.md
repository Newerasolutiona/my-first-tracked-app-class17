# Calculator

This is a simple calculator using shell scripting.

## How to use

- To run the calculator, execute `./calculator.sh` in a terminal.
- You will see a menu with five options: Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division and Exit.
- Enter your choice by typing a number from 1 to 5 and pressing enter.
- Enter the first and second numbers when prompted and press enter.
- You will see the result of your calculation.
- You can choose to continue or exit by typing y or n and pressing enter.
